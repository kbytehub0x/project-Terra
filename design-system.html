container-max: 1280px --- ## Brand & Style The design system is built on a foundation of **Modern Corporate** aesthetics infused with a **Supportive Legal Tech** narrative. It aims to evoke a sense of absolute reliability, protection, and clarity for gig economy workers navigating complex legal or financial landscapes. The style prioritizes high-legibility, structured information density, and a "calm-tech" interface that reduces cognitive load. We utilize a refined professional aesthetic—clean lines, purposeful whitespace, and a high-contrast palette—to signal authority and trustworthiness. The emotional response should be one of "relief" and "confidence," moving away from the chaotic nature of gig work toward a structured, protected environment. ## Colors The palette is anchored by **Deep Navy (#0F172A)**, representing the institutional stability of legal services. This is contrasted against a **Crisp White and Slate-Tinted Background (#F8FAFC)** to maintain a clean, modern dashboard feel. - **Primary (Navy):** Used for navigation, headers, and primary actions to establish hierarchy. - **Secondary (Action Blue):** A bright, professional blue used for interactive elements and highlights. - **Success (Emerald):** Used sparingly for "Rescue" status, resolutions, and positive affirmations. - **Neutrals (Slate/Grey):** Orchestrated to manage information density, with darker shades for body text and lighter shades for borders and secondary metadata. ## Typography This design system utilizes **Inter** exclusively to leverage its systematic, utilitarian, and highly readable qualities. The typographic scale is optimized for data-heavy dashboards and legal documentation. - **Headlines:** Use tighter letter-spacing and heavier weights to create a sense of importance and firm structure. - **Body Text:** Standard weight (400) is used for maximum legibility in long-form legal explanations or case details. - **Labels:** Small caps or increased letter-spacing are applied to labels to differentiate them from interactive text and body content. ## Layout & Spacing The layout follows a **structured fluid grid** model. On desktop, the dashboard utilizes a persistent left sidebar (280px) with a fluid content area that caps at 1280px to maintain line-length readability. - **Grid:** A 12-column system is used for the main content area, allowing for flexible card layouts (1/2, 1/3, and 2/3 spans). - **Rhythm:** An 8px linear scale governs all padding and margins to ensure visual harmony. - **Mobile:** Elements reflow to a single column with 16px horizontal margins. Complex tables convert to card-based views to maintain accessibility on small screens. ## Elevation & Depth To maintain a professional and clean aesthetic, this design system uses **Tonal Layers** supplemented by **Ambient Shadows**. We avoid heavy gradients in favor of subtle depth cues. - **Level 0 (Background):** Solid `#F8FAFC` to provide a clean canvas. - **Level 1 (Cards/Containers):** Pure white background with a 1px border in `#E2E8F0` and a very soft, diffused shadow (`0 4px 6px -1px rgb(0 0 0 / 0.05)`). - **Level 2 (Popovers/Modals):** Increased shadow depth to indicate high-priority interaction, using a double-layered shadow for a natural "lifted" effect. - **Contrast Outlines:** Interactive form fields use a subtle 1px border that shifts from neutral grey to Primary Navy upon focus, avoiding heavy drop shadows to keep the UI "crisp." ## Shapes The shape language is **Rounded**, striking a balance between the rigidity of legal structures and the approachability of a support service. - **Standard Elements:** Buttons, input fields, and small cards use a **0.5rem (8px)** radius. - **Large Containers:** Dashboard widgets and main content sections use a **1rem (16px)** radius to soften the overall appearance of the page. - **Icons:** Should be housed in slightly rounded square containers or used as "naked" line-art to maintain the clean, technical feel. ## Components Consistent styling of components ensures the "Legal Tech" narrative is maintained across the entire user journey. - **Buttons:** - **Primary:** Solid Navy background, white text, 8px radius. High-impact for "Start Claim" or "Submit." - **Secondary:** White background, Navy border (1px), Navy text. Used for "Cancel" or "Save Draft." - **Input Fields:** Use a light grey background (`#F1F5F9`) with a 1px border. On focus, the border transitions to Action Blue with a 2px outer glow (ring). Labels are always positioned above the field for clarity. - **Status Chips:** Small, semi-transparent background tints with high-contrast text (e.g., a pale green background with dark green text for "Resolved"). - **Cards:** Used for case summaries and earnings insights. They feature a 1px border and the "Level 1" ambient shadow. - **Progress Steppers:** Horizontal indicators used for legal filing processes, using Navy for completed steps and Slate for upcoming steps, ensuring the user always knows their "location" in a complex process.
